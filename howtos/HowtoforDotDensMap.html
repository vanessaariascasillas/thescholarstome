How to Dot Density and Swipe Two Maps

Please try out these steps to give you a better idea of how your project steps will be. I am using the attributes “Male” and “Females” for this How To.

Step 1:

Add Layers from ArcGIS Online Library

Presidential Election Results_WFL1

PLAN C2193 National Congressional Districts TX

Step 2:

Use tool named Join Features from the Analysis Button

Step 3:

Click on layer you made and go to Styles pick Dot Density and the fields should be Males and Females

Step 4:

Change Dot Style Options, click Done or it will not save

Step 5:

Turn off all Layers but the ones you will be presenting

Step 6:

Save Map, then click on Instant Apps

Step 7:

Choose Basic (Media Map)

Step 8:

Click on Interactivity then click on swipe tool, under trailing layers click both layers on. Have fun swiping!
